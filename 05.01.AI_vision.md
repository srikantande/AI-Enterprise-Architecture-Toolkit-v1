# AI Vision & Strategy

## 1. Vision Statement
> **“Leverage AI to drive a 10 % revenue uplift through predictive insights and automated decision‑making across the enterprise.”**

## 2. Business Objectives
- Increase customer retention by 8 %  
- Reduce operational cost by 12 %  
- Accelerate time‑to‑market for new services by 30 %

## 3. Scope
- Predictive maintenance for critical assets  
- Demand forecasting for high‑volume SKUs  
- Fraud detection for financial transactions  

## 4. Success Metrics
| Metric | Target | Measurement |
|--------|--------|-------------|
| Model F1‑Score | ≥ 0.85 | Quarterly model evaluation |
| Cost per inference | ≤ $0.001 | Cloud billing reports |
| Model drift detection | ≤ 2 % deviation | Automated drift alerts |
